ZipPoint Star

Puzzle game with unique mechanics

Role Game Developer
Year 2024
Tools Unity, C#

Overview

ZipPoint Star is an engaging 2D puzzle game that challenges players with unique mechanics and creative level design. The game features innovative gameplay elements that keep players entertained while testing their problem-solving skills.

The Challenge

Creating a puzzle game that is both accessible to newcomers and challenging for experienced players. The main challenge was designing mechanics that feel fresh and intuitive while maintaining engaging gameplay throughout all levels.

The Solution

Developed a unique star-collection mechanic combined with zip-line movement system. This allows for creative level design and varied puzzle solutions, keeping the gameplay fresh and exciting.

Key Features

  • Unique zip-line movement mechanic
  • 50+ challenging levels
  • Progressive difficulty curve
  • Colorful and engaging visuals
  • Smooth controls and responsive gameplay
